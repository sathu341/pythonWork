list,tuple,set,dictionary

eg:
a=[]
a=[1,2,3]
a=['apple','apple','orange','Grape']
a[0]='bannana']
tuple=> not changeable,duplicate value allow
eg:
b=()
b=(34,56,7)
b[0]=54

set=>not allow duplication
set=>{}
eg:
set{'apple','orange','apple'}
unordered
